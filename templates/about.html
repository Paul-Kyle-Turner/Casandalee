About

This is the ttrpg rules lawyer that I named "Casandalee". It's goal is to give you accurate information about TTRPG games. Right now it only has embedded the information for the Pathfinder 2e game. Monsters and NPC's will have to wait for a larger release.

Generative Ai is powerful but not always right. Be sure to check with the books or your games documentation to be 100% sure.

Example Message:

How many actions is rage?

Rage is a 1-action activity.
